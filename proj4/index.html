Shadow Hero

Instructions

Please Read Instructions before playing.

Press Enter to Advance or initate dialogue.

In battle, use W to move the pointer up and S to move the pointer down. Press ENTER to make a selection.

Some enemies are faster than you so they will finish their action before you do.

All enemies have a small chance to miss their attacks against you.

All enemies will make the player stronger in some way, even if the changes arent readily apparent.

The SKILL option in combat temporarily doubles your attack power but costs a small amount of health on each attack. It only works once, so spamming it is useless.

There are NPCs in each town that will heal the player.

The player has been blessed by the goddess of plenty, use HEAL in combat will restore health up to the current armor level. (no limit)

Looking for a challenge? Try and beat the game by defeating as few enemies as possible.

WARNING: If you attempt to run from any bosses, then the Shadow King will win and the world will be consumed by darkness. This includes enemies that block progression.
